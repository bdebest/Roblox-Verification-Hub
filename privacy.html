Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collected through the Leeds Karting verification and moderation systems across Discord and Roblox.

1. What We Collect

  • Roblox username and user ID (via OAuth)
  • Discord user ID and username
  • Group rank or roles associated with your Roblox or Discord accounts

2. How We Use It

  • To verify user identity between platforms
  • To assign Discord roles based on Roblox group rank
  • To log moderation actions (e.g., kicks, bans)
  • To ensure fair access to game features and community tools

3. Data Protection & Compliance

  • We comply with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and GDPR.
  • No passwords or sensitive personal data are stored or collected.
  • We do not share or sell your data to third parties.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for security or access control.

4. Contact

If you have any questions or would like your data removed, please contact a Leeds Karting admin through our Discord server.
